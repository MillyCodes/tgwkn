The Girl Who Knew Nothing


TGWKN is a project by me, Milly. I'm learning to code my own webpages and yes, it's full of struggle.

After playing with coding as a hobby most of my life I have finally decided that it may be worth it to actually formally learn HTML, CSS and even more importantly JavaScript and JQuery. It was fun and easy at first however, the truth is IT'S FUCKING HARD. You won't find me smiling and acting as if life smells like roses while I'm learning because I am actually frustrated but I am also determined to learn this shit. Ultimately, I know it's worth it.
